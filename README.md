## Real Estate Analytics & Project Performance Platform

## Project Overview

The Real Estate Analytics & Project Performance Platform is an enterprise-grade business intelligence solution designed to provide end-to-end visibility into real estate operations, including sales performance, inventory management, customer analytics, collections monitoring, project execution, and revenue forecasting.

The platform integrates data from CRM systems, ERP platforms, sales databases, and financial systems into a centralized analytics ecosystem, enabling leadership teams to monitor project health, sales trends, inventory movement, collections efficiency, and overall business performance in real time.

---

## Business Problem

Real estate organizations manage large volumes of operational, financial, and customer data across multiple projects, locations, and business units. However, information is often fragmented across different systems, making it difficult to obtain a unified view of project and business performance.

Common business challenges include:

- Limited visibility into project sales performance.
- Difficulty tracking inventory utilization and availability.
- Delayed collections and payment monitoring.
- Lack of customer acquisition and conversion insights.
- Manual reporting and reconciliation processes.
- Challenges in forecasting project revenues and demand.

### Common Business Questions

- Which projects are performing above or below targets?
- What is the current inventory status across projects?
- How are sales conversions trending over time?
- Which customer segments contribute most to revenue?
- What is the collections status and outstanding receivables position?
- Which projects require immediate management attention?

Without integrated analytics, strategic planning and operational decision-making become significantly more difficult.

---

## Solution

Developed a centralized real estate analytics platform integrating sales, inventory, customer, finance, collections, and project management data into a single reporting ecosystem.

Built automated ETL pipelines and analytical models using SQL and Python to process large-scale operational datasets. Developed interactive Power BI dashboards enabling stakeholders to monitor project sales performance, inventory movement, booking trends, customer acquisition metrics, collections efficiency, revenue performance, and project-level KPIs.

Implemented forecasting models to predict sales demand, revenue trends, inventory utilization, and collection performance. Designed executive scorecards, drill-down reports, and exception monitoring frameworks to support proactive decision-making.

The platform provided real-time visibility into business operations while significantly reducing manual reporting efforts.
